#CoMoments

Discovering the best of Columbia, one story at a time
VOX STAFF


We all have favorite Columbia moments — the special memories that stand out above the rest. Maybe it's when you shared your first kiss with the person you love. Or the last kiss with the person you hate. Maybe it's when you had your first slice of Shakes pizza sitting in Peace Park or when you shared a beer with your favorite literary icon who happened to be in town. Vox surveyed members of the Columbia community to find out just what those moments are and where they took place. Click on the markers around the map to find out a little more.
